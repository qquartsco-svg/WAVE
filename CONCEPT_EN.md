# Wave Propagation Foundation — Concept

Everything starts from one equation:

```
∂²u/∂t² = c²∇²u
```

Change c and you get sound, seismic waves, surface waves, light, gravitational waves.
Add damping and you get vibration decay.
Add nonlinearity and you get shock waves.

This foundation covers that starting point:
1. General wave → wavelength, frequency, energy, attenuation
2. Oscillator → resonance, Q-factor, decay
3. Seismic → P/S/surface wave tracking, epicenter triangulation
4. Surface & acoustic → dispersion, deep/shallow water, acoustic impedance
5. Gravitational waves → strain, chirp mass, LIGO/LISA sensitivity

Current scope: **screening foundation** — not full numerical simulation, but rapid readability of wave phenomena.
